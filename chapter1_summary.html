Symmetric Field Physics — Summary

“In the Beginning” — the foundational chapter that establishes the entire cosmology.
Everything in the remaining 11 chapters builds upon these laws.

Section 1 — In the Beginning

The ONE Divides into Two

The universe originates from a singular MIND (God, ALL THAT IS, ONE) — a single point and continuum. From this unity comes the first and most fundamental act: a division into two equal and opposite forces — a contracting force (a point) and an expansive force (a sphere). This is the LAW OF CREATION.

The symbol for this relationship is >< — equal and opposite, joined at a fulcrum of power. Every subsequent division of energy follows this same pattern: always in halves, always equal and opposite, always connected through a balancing fulcrum. Each division is called an octave — a group of resonant speeds.

First Principle We live in a ONE self-creating, self-dividing Universe. No thing, no action, no life, no idea is separate from the ONE. All is a function of ONE in symmetric expression. From this system arises the study of Alchemical Law.
Section 2 — Space Field = Time Field

The Two Fundamental Substances

The two halves of the ONE are named and defined. They are not abstractions — they are substances, as real as any material, just far finer than human senses can detect.

S Field — Space Field

  • Kinetic expression of MIND — forms the sphere
  • Expands outward in all directions with equal pressure
  • Accelerates outward to the limits of its sphere
  • This expansion is called RADIATION
  • Always repels itself (internal pressure, not repulsion)
  • Example: gas filling a balloon

T Field — Time Field

  • Potential expression of MIND — forms the point
  • Contracts inward from a spacious position to a line to a point
  • Decelerates inward to the limits of its speed
  • This contraction is called CLOSURE
  • Always attracts itself to the smallest possible place
  • Held at a PLACE for a DURATION
Absolute Rule There is never an action in one field without an equal and opposite action in the other. The S Field and T Field will NOT fuse, but always stand side by side. They are absolute barriers to one another. There is no place where pairs of ST Fields do not exist. There is no emptiness and no chaos.

An important clarification: "repelling" is not a principle law the way CLOSURE is. S Field expands by internal pressure — it does not push T Field away. T Field does not push S Field away but demands to stand side by side in balance. When one field comes forcefully against the other, it is stopped and diverted — not repelled or annihilated. A T Field point or line is always surrounded by a swirling S Field sheath.

Section 3 — Point of Change = 0

The Breathing Cycle of Transformation

The chapter introduces the critical concept of the "point of change = 0" — the dynamic event where one field transforms into the other.

The S Field radiates outward from a T Field center point. At the circumference of its sphere, it reaches maximum speed, and at that exact place and speed it reaches a "point of change = 0" — where S Field turns itself into T Field. Now as T Field, it becomes a line and travels back to center.

Conversely, as T Field lines contract inward, they slow down, begin to coil, and at the center limit they reach another "point of change = 0" — where T Field changes to S Field and begins its outward movement once more.

The Breathing The ONE expresses periodically in an in-breath and an out-breath, alternating the symmetric aspects of the ONE force. This is true for the gravitational forces around the earth and all other cosmic bodies.
Section 4 — Time & Space as Substance

Both Fields Are Real Substances — Energy Equals Material

The chapter makes a bold assertion: both Time and Space are substances. T Field is the bonding force in the design of structures. It “spends” time in cycles of duration — this is what you measure as “half-life.” Time you spend every day is an energy currency. Your DNA has units of T Field designed into its structure that express in cycles of living flesh and bone.

The ST Field corpuscles are so fine they express as a continuous ocean of energy. They pass through you like you were a sieve — you swim in them like a fish in a sea. Only when the ST Field becomes very compressed, very dense, and tightly bonded does it appear as a solid, defined by barriers.

Key Insight Energy and materials are the same entity. The Space-Time Fields come together to formulate action in cycles. As T Field slows down, its linear bonds tighten — it becomes cold and hard and its time extends into duration.

Time is determined by the master clocks of the galaxy and solar system, yet each living thing experiences time individually. Time is absolutely relative to speed. Very small differences in speed show great varieties of material manifestation.

Section 5 — Speed, Octaves & Temperature

Fields Are Defined by Speed — Hot and Cold Measure Field States

Your solar system has a fixed average speed base. Everything traveling together — rotation, orbit, atomic vibration, metabolic rates — gives a resultant speed much higher than you realize. Even small differences in speed produce vast varieties of material forms.

Octaves overlap as overtones and undertones. The suns and planets you see are overlaid with suns and planets you cannot see. Two or more things DO exist in the same place at the same time.

Temperature is a direct measurement of field speed. Cold = slower speed, higher potential (T Field contraction/CLOSURE). Hot = higher speed, greater kinetic energy (S Field expansion). Water expresses in three octaves — liquid, gas, and frozen solid — as examples of octave divisions within one system.

Octave Access There is the difficult concept of speeds relative to zero where the quality of zero varies. A compendium octave change means a system change. That system shift is accessed through a harmonic geodesic concordance.
Section 6 — Axes, Corpuscles & Harmonics

The Architecture of the Field

Axes are not imaginary but actually present as highways of energy travel. Every sphere has three axes (X, Y, Z) perpendicular to each other, defining meridian and equatorial planes. Radii act as a multitude of axes — all are avenues for complex circuitry.

The corpuscular field follows precise laws of division:

How Corpuscles Divide

  • Any ST Field divides itself into halves (undertone octaves) along axial lines
  • Any ST Field doubles itself into twins (overtone octaves) along axial lines
  • Each corpuscle has a unique “address in space”
  • Corpuscles do NOT move about unless disturbed
  • There is NOT a fixed amount of energy — new energy is “born” every day
  • No energy is lost, but it can change octave

Harmonic Division

  • A radius intersecting a sphere's interior surface creates new centers
  • These harmonically repeat, producing 12-sided figures
  • Cross sections reveal hexagrams — seen in crystals
  • Axial intersections produce cubes and 8-sided figures
  • Corpuscles fill all space — crushed together like bubbles
  • Most commonly form cubes and parallelepipeds
Law of Octaves Overtones can CREATE undertones. Overtones can absorb undertones. Undertones, by themselves, cannot effect overtones. In groups and harmonies, undertones CAN alter overtones.
Section 7 — Networks & Creative Union

How Circuits Form and Matter Is Created

Networks: Any T Field line or plane intersecting another T Field line or plane will form a potential point of radiation at the intersection. The combined potential at that point develops an appropriate sphere with axes. T Field lines between every T Field point of similar magnitude form pathways — circuits — that join with each other and form a “ground” to a major center of ONE.

Creative Union: When two spheres of the same octave intersect, they form a common plane always perpendicular to the line between their two centers. It is upon this plane that energies unite to bond matter. It is here where both the lower and higher fields merge with MIND — where planning and materialization occur. A sphere develops at the point of intersection between every point, line, or surface — creating an unending fan of overtones and undertones.

Compression & Compassion: Every atom demonstrates its own peculiar harmonic characteristic. An atom's nature cannot be identified by its “parts and pieces” but by its internal and imposed harmonies. The chapter offers its first law of alchemy: to understand an atom, be a loving master — like understanding a pet, you must listen to it gently, not bang about on it in an unnatural force field.

Section 8 — Universal Fields (USUT)

The Grand Overtone That Doubles Everything

Above the ST Fields exists a greater octave system: the Universal Space Field (US) and Universal Time Field (UT) — together called the USUT Fields. These prefer to exercise their influence along the plane of tangency between paired corpuscles.

Properties of the USUT Fields

  • Radius double any group of smaller ST Field octaves
  • Electromagnetic speeds double what we know
  • Wavelengths can be twice as long, amplitude twice as great
  • Stabilize the sun, earth, and all galactic environments
  • Act as the balancing fulcrum between S and T Fields
  • Double any force, action, or signal in our fields
  • Inertia and Momentum are the direct result of interlocking USUT Fields
  • One field easily passes through the other — unless it elects to lock into gear

The chapter uses the piano analogy: imagine a piano twice as big as yours. When its middle C is played, you can't hear it directly, but your smaller piano will lock into the vibration and faintly resonate. The galaxy is our biggest overtone; the sun is the first overtone we can identify with; the earth's vibrational breath echoes in our bodies.

The Only Universal Language The only vibration that bridges all octaves is that of unqualified love. That vibration can be used as a carrier wave. — “As above, so below; as below, so above” is a truism.
Section 9 — Energy Equals Substance

How Matter Is Formed from Fields

When S Field and T Field are bound together in configuration, they are surrounded by a unifying sphere with surface tension — tightly compacted ST Field corpuscles that form a barrier boundary. This is what we call substance.

Beneath the surface tension, a network of T Field radii CLOSE inward to center. Each union is resisted by equal and opposite S Field. The result: an agreement called material. Every material has a “half-life” — the continual force of CLOSURE causes material breakdown with such regularity it can be used as a clock.

Creation The union of ST Fields is the act of creation. Unions usually occur in a condition of fire — harmonic speeds interlock the ST Field in very specific conformations. A variety of Universal circuits are also called fires and are specifically applied in the designs of creation events authorized by the Divine ONE.
Section 10 — Signals Traverse the Fields

The Cascade Effect — Signals Do Not Travel, They Reflect

This is one of the chapter's most radical propositions. Alternating S and T corpuscles packed like a checkerboard form a continuum. An action in one corpuscle reflects to its partner, then to the next, and the next — the “cascade effect.” The exact essence of an event is reflected everywhere.

Light does not travel from the sun to your eye. A photon (A) generates a light signal on the sun. That signal meets its destination and is measured as a new photon (B). Photon A and B seem to be the same, but they are two photons in different places. Energy is created and transformed every second. Signals do not travel through space — they transit across space via reflection through the field continuum.

Signal Law Any action in one S Field predominant corpuscle will be reflected, equally and opposite, in the adjacent T Field predominant corpuscle. Signals create illusions. Signals can create substantial illusion. Using the pure energy of the ST Field, harmonic signal configurations master-mind the creation of new energy in the likeness of the old.

The sun is not hurling photons across millions of miles. Most sunlight events never leave the sun but are accurately replicated through the spatial continuum and reactivated within your being and your plants. This is why the sun will not “burn itself out” — it is not giving up all its energy to outer space and has its own continuous fuel supply.

Section 11 — Adaptive Biology & Unity

Living Signals & The Law of Unity

The chapter concludes with a remarkable claim about biology: living creatures change themselves dramatically through signal intelligence. A butterfly whose wings look like flowers literally communicated a desire to a greater intelligence who assisted its DNA change. Desire propels a signal to where it needs to go. Humans use the same process but are slowed by traditions and lack of trust.

The Law of Unity closes the chapter: unity implies synthesis, synchronicity, involvement, and centralization. The T Field mechanically attracts itself, yet its inseparability from the expanding S Field is a symmetric unity. In the center of unified action is stillness — the essence of unity. Separation from unity does not exist from the standpoint of Root Cause. Imbalance stimulates the forces of balance.

Closing Law The dual fields are forever justified on the fulcrum of the Truth of Unity. Purpose and Cause are the vocabulary of this truth — as are the words Love, Wonder, Beauty, and also Disarrangement and Disaffiliation.
